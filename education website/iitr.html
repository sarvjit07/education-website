IIT Roorkee Courses


IIT Roorkee through its 23 academic departments and 7 centers offers a total of 14 courses. The core areas of study include Engineering, Applied Sciences, Humanities, & Social Sciences, and Management. Admission to all the courses is done via national-level entrance scores only. At the UG level, the institute offers 4 years BTech, 5 years BArch, and dual degree programs. Whereas, IIT Roorkee offers MTech, MDes, MArch, MBA, MSc, and PhD programs at the postgraduate level. Additionally, IIT Roorkee has also started offering PG diploma and advanced certificate programs in Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI. IIT Roorkee Admission


IIT Roorkee admission 2022 is based on national-level entrance exams. Admission to the BTech program is based on the JEE Advanced scores. The exam will be conducted on July 3, 2022. Currently, JEE Main 2022 registrations for the April session is ongoing. Candidates can fill the application form till March 31. The examination for the same will be held from April 16-21, 2022. IIT Roorkee MTech admissions are based on GATE exam scores. The examination concluded on Feb 13. GATE 2022 results will be announced on March 17. IIT Roorkee Ranking

Apart from being one of the oldest IITs in the country, IIT Roorkee also belong to the league of top IITs in the nation. The quality and richness of the institution are very well reflected in its rankings. In the list of of QS World University Rankings 2022, IIT Roorkee has been ranked at 400. Apart from this, the institute is also ranked at 7 in the Overall and 6 in the Engineering category by the NIRF 2021 rankings. IIT Roorkee is also ranked in the Best Colleges category released by India Today 2021 rankings. The institute has been ranked at 1 for Architecture under the Department of Architecture and Planning. Like every year, IIT Roorkee has maintained its position at spot 6 in the NIRF Ranking 2020. The institute has consistently been allotted 6th rank since the year 2016. IIT Roorkee falls in the parameters of top 10 engineering institutions in the other ranking list of Outlook-ICARE India, India Today and The Week.
