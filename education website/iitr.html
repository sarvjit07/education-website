IIT Roorkee has over 8000 students, 470 faculty members, and 250 patents filed. The institute is one of the biggest technical institutes in India with 23 academic departments, 3 centres of excellence, 5 academic service centres, and 3 supporting units. The institute offers 83 courses in the fields of Engineering, Applied Sciences, Humanities, Social Sciences, and Management. The flagship program of the institute is BTech, specialized in 13 streams. The selection to the program requires a JEE Advanced rank between 32 to 7000. IIT Roorkee fee for the program is INR 1,48,000 per semester for the Gen/OBC category students. The amount includes INR 1 Lac as the tuition fee which is payable only by the Gen and OBC category students. The institute exempts SC/ST/PD category students from the payment of tuition fee. For the IIT Roorkee BTech Admission 2022, NTA has announced JEE Main 2022 dates. The examination will be conducted in two Phases. JEE Main 2022 exam date for the Phase 1 session is April 16-21, 2022. Dates for the Phase 2 session are May 24-29, 2022. The registrations for the April session are open now. Last date to apply is March 31. In addition to 83 courses, IIT Roorkee also offers special PG programs to Afro Asan Region Students. The programs are offered by the Water Resources Development and Management (WRDM) and Department of Hydrology, IIT Roorkee. Till date, the department has trained over 2469 students as Service Engineers and Agricultural scientists from 48 countries. The courses offered are sponsored by the Govt of India and UNESCO.
