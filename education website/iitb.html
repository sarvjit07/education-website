IIT Bombay is among the top 3 engineering colleges in India. The institute offers 139 courses in Engineering, Management, Pure Sciences, and Humanities. IIT Bombay courses include regular degree programs such as UG, PG, and PhD programs in Engineering, Pure Sciences, and Management. The institute also offers Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) through an online platform, IITBombayX. These courses are imparted through 34 centres, 17 academic departments, and 3 schools. Amongst the 139 courses offered, IIT Bombay BTech is the flagship program of the institute. Admission to the course requires 1-2000 rank in the JEE Advanced exam. The total fee for the 4-year BTech program is INR 9.18 Lacs. For the IIT Bombay BTech Admission 2022, NTA has announced JEE Main 2022 exam dates. The exam is to be conducted in two sessions this year. First session of JEE Main 2022 will be conducted from April 16-21, 2022. While the second session will be conducted from May 24-29, 2022. Candidates can fill JEE Main 2022 application form for the April session till March 31. Also, the NTA has removed minimum 75% eligibility criteria for the JEE Main 2022 exam. Another program that is much popular amongst the aspirants is the IIT Bombay MTech program. Admission to the program is based on the GATE exam. IIT Bombay Cutoff for the MTech program is 380-550 scores in the GATE exam. IIT Bombay fees for the 2-year program is INR 1.39 Lacs.

IIT Bombay Courses


IIT Bombay Courses include UG, PG, Dual-degree, and Doctoral degrees offered through 86+ courses. The courses offered cover major streams such as Engineering, Management, Humanities, and Pure Sciences. The UG courses of the institute include BTech, BDes, BS, degrees with 4-years of duration. And, the PG courses of the institute include MTech, MDes, MBA, and MSc degree with 2-years of duration. Admission to all the courses is based on national-level examinations. IIT Bombay Admission


IIT Bombay admission 2022 is primarily entrance-based. Admission to IIT Bombay BTech program is offered through Joint Entrance Examination (JEE). NTA has announced the JEE Main 2022 dates. The exam will be conducted in two sessions from April 16-21 and May 24-29, 2022. For the April session exam, JEE Main 2022 application form are out. Candidates can fill the application form till March 31. Final selection for admission will be made via JEE Advanced exam, scheduled for July 3. IIT Bombay MTech admission is done through GATE exam scores. The examination concluded on Feb 13. GATE 2022 results will be announced on March 17. IIT Bombay offers 86 courses at the UG, PG, and Doctoral levels. Selection of applicants to these courses is done on the basis of national-level entrance exams. In addition to this, the institute also offers video and text-based short-term certificate courses. Given below are the updates on IIT Bombay admissions 2022:
