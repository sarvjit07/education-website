Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV) is a state university situated in Bhopal, India. The university is recognized by the UGC and accredited with an "A" Grade by the NAAC. RGPV is formerly known as the State Technological University of Madhya Pradesh with a campus sprawling over 247-acres. At present, the university is offering UG, PG, Diploma/Polytechnic, and Research level across 400+ RGPV affiliated colleges and institutions. It has 5 UTD's, 200 affiliated Engineering colleges, 98 pharmacy colleges, 95 MCA colleges, 4 Architecture Colleges, and 85 polytechnic institutions. B.E/BTech is the flagship program of the RGPV Bhopal. Admissions to the same are offered on the basis of JEE Main scores followed by counseling conducted by the university. RGPV cutoff for the B.E/B.Tech program ranges from 32781-738471 marks for the general category candidates. Apart from the B.E program, the university also offers various other popular programs such as B.Arch, B.Pharm, MBA, MCA, M.Pharm, M.E, and dual degrees, etc. Admission to these programs is based on both national-level entrance scores and merit lists. These online programs can be accessed by anyone with the presence of extensive internet facilities anywhere in the world. In addition to this, some of the affiliated colleges of RGPV have also been permitted to impart post-graduate teaching in the new domains of knowledge. RGPV offers great placement and internship opportunities to its students. Every year more than 80% of the graduates are placed in top-notch companies with impressive salary packages. Some of the top recruiters are BYJUS, Quikr, OLX, Wipro, TCS, Accenture, Tech Mahindra, Infosys, MuSigma, etc. RGPV has recently signed several MoUs and entered into collaborations with a number of prestigious institutions and organizations such as ICAR-IISS, NASSCOM, MPBOU, Dauto Engineering Pvt. Ltd., etc., to promote student training and offer industry oriented certification programs.
